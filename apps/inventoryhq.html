Inventory HQ

Offline inventory, sales, employees and a decision engine — for the small shop running on one PC.
On the Store Decision engine Works offline No subscription Windows 10 / 11
Get it on the Microsoft Store →

What it does

Most small-business inventory tools are either oversimplified ("just a list of items") or sold as $50–$150-a-month SaaS subscriptions that need a cloud account, a login and a steady internet connection. Inventory HQ sits between the two. It runs natively on Windows, stores everything in a single local database on your PC, and gives a small shop the operational visibility — items, sales, employees, tools, monthly reports — that bigger systems charge a recurring fee for.

The Dashboard is what makes it different. As you record sales it builds a daily report, compares this month to the last saved one, and shows a plain-English recommendation in the Decision Engine panel — so you don't have to interpret the numbers yourself.

Five modules, one shop

📊

Dashboard

Today's items sold, max/min sale, low-stock count, plus investment, return and profit for today and the current month. Comparison vs the last saved report and a one-line recommendation from the Decision Engine.

📦

Items

Add products with name, manufacturer, cost, selling price, quantity and unit. Search by ID or name, update individual fields, delete with confirmation, and sell from the same screen — quantity reduces automatically.

💰

Sales

Every sale is stored with cost and price snapshotted at the moment it happened, so historical profit stays accurate even if you later change an item's price. Search by ID, name or manufacturer; update or delete records as needed.

🔧

Tools

Track the physical equipment the shop owns — scanners, printers, registers — with quantity and condition. Quick add, search, update and delete, with confirmation prompts on destructive actions.

👥

Employees, Tasks & Hours

Staff roster with phone, email, position and date hired. Assign tasks with status and due/completion dates, and log daily hours per employee — name and position fetched automatically from their ID.

🧠

Decision Engine

Compares the current month to the previous saved report and surfaces one of three plain-English recommendations — scale top performers, review pricing, or monitor — so you act on the data instead of just reading it.

Why offline matters

  • Your products, sales, staff and pricing live on your PC — not on someone else's server
  • Works when the internet doesn't — busy days are the worst time for a cloud tool to drop
  • One-time purchase, no monthly fee, no per-seat pricing
  • One file is your backup — copy it to a USB drive and you're protected

A look inside

Dashboard — Today & This Month — items sold, max/min sale, low-stock count and investment / return / profit for today and the current month.
Comparison & Decision Engine — sale and profit change vs the last saved report, with a plain-English recommendation.
Top Manufacturer & Top 10 Rankings — best manufacturer by units and profit, plus most/least sold and most/least profitable items this month.
Saved Reports — every daily snapshot kept on file, searchable by date.
Items — add, search, update, sell and delete products, with a live table of everything in stock.
Sales — full sale history with cost, price, quantity, profit and date — searchable by ID, name or manufacturer.
Tools — the shop's physical equipment with quantity and condition, kept separate from sellable stock.
Employees — staff roster with phone, email, position and date hired. Add, search, update or remove.
Tasks — assign tasks to employees with status and dates, then mark them on-going or completed.
Hours — log daily hours per employee; name and position are pulled in automatically from the employee ID.

Good to know

Inventory HQ is a native WPF app for Windows 10 and 11. All data is stored locally in %LOCALAPPDATA%\InventoryHQ\InventoryHQ.db — a single SQLite file you can copy anywhere to back it up. No accounts, no telemetry, no network calls. Designed for shops with a handful of staff and a few hundred SKUs. Available on the Microsoft Store.
